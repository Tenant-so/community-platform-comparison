## Platform Profiles

### Tenant.so

[Tenant.so](https://tenant.so) is an all-in-one community operating system. It combines community spaces, online courses, built-in email marketing, real-time chat, events, gamification (streaks, recognition badges, leagues), an AI assistant, automation, and flexible monetization — including cards, BNPL, ACH, and crypto — all at 0% platform fees.

**Best for:** Creators and businesses who want to replace their entire SaaS stack (Circle + ConvertKit + Zapier + course host) with a single platform.

**Standout features:**
- Multi-tenant architecture (run multiple communities from one account)
- Built-in email campaigns (no need for ConvertKit/Mailchimp)
- Crypto, BNPL, and ACH payments at 0% platform fee
- AI-powered course assistant (Jarvis)
- 31+ integrations built in
- Real-time chat powered by Ably

### Circle

[Circle](https://circle.so) is a community platform with spaces, courses, and events. Popular with creators and brands.

**Best for:** Creators who want a clean community experience with course capabilities.

**Limitations:** No built-in email. No real-time chat. No gamification. Extra charges for media storage. Recent price increases have frustrated many users.

### Skool

[Skool](https://skool.com) is a simple community + course platform with gamification. Founded by Sam Ovens.

**Best for:** Course creators who want simplicity and a built-in leaderboard.

**Limitations:** No custom domain. No events. No API. No email. No chat. Limited customization. Single $99/mo plan.

### Kajabi

[Kajabi](https://kajabi.com) is an all-in-one platform for courses, coaching, memberships, and email marketing.

**Best for:** Course creators who want email marketing and sales funnels built in.

**Limitations:** Community features are basic. No real-time chat. No gamification. Expensive (starts at $149/mo). No crypto/BNPL payments.

### Mighty Networks

[Mighty Networks](https://mightynetworks.com) is a community platform with courses, events, and a native mobile app.

**Best for:** Community builders who want a branded mobile app.

**Limitations:** 2-3% platform fee on transactions. Limited API. AI features are new and basic.

### Teachable

[Teachable](https://teachable.com) is a course platform with basic community features.

**Best for:** Educators focused primarily on course delivery.

**Limitations:** Community features are an afterthought. Free plan takes 10% + $1 per sale. No real-time chat. No events.

### Heartbeat

[Heartbeat](https://heartbeat.chat) is a chat-first community platform with events and threads.

**Best for:** Teams and communities that prioritize real-time conversation over content.

**Limitations:** No courses. No email. No gamification. No payments/monetization.

### Discord

[Discord](https://discord.com) is a free chat platform with voice channels, roles, and bots.

**Best for:** Gaming, crypto, and developer communities. Budget-friendly option.

**Limitations:** Not designed for monetization. No courses. No email. No SEO visibility. Hard to organize content. Community feels "hidden" behind login.
